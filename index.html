CS Thesis

Practical Uses of Blockchain:

Current State and Emerging Trends

Description

Blockchain is a form of Distributed Ledger Technology where information is stored in chained blocks. This approach results in two key characteristics: the immutability of transactions, and the consequent ease of verifying their authenticity. This study has been conducted with the aim of analyzing this technology, going beyond the popular applications in the world of finance, delving into deeper topics such as their applicability in the supply chain, in the healthcare sector, or in the field of computing.
 

Tags

DLT Blockchain Tokens Smart Contracts

BBA Thesis

Digital Currencies:

Are CBDCs the Future?

Description

Nowadays everyone is highly exposed to the internet, whether through a mobile phone, a computer, or any other type of electronic device. Currently, online payments are carried out through private entities with private money, not fully backed by a central bank. This study has been conducted with the aim of analyzing the reasons and implications involved in issuing the alternative that multiple governments are researching and/or developing: the CBDCs or Central Bank Digital Currencies.
